# Ecological Interactions and Biological Control (Andow, Ragsdale, Nyvall, eds., 1997)

Westview Press (Routledge reprint 2019). 349 pages, 20 chapters, 3 parts. Conference-proceedings style, so chapters are self-contained research contributions. Cite by chapter number + author surname(s). Grep `andow_biocontrol_full_text.md` for exact passages.

**Stance:** This is the corpus's **biological-control / tritrophic specialist**. It covers classical, augmentative, and conservation biocontrol across insects, weeds, and plant pathogens. Prefer this source whenever the claim under review is about predators, parasitoids, entomopathogens, weed biocontrol, or disease suppression — Gliessman gives these topics only a chapter of coverage; Andow et al. give them a book.
